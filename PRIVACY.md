# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 16, 2026**

## Overview

Takeout Cleaner Pro is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

## Data Collection

**We collect ZERO data.**

Takeout Cleaner Pro:
- ✅ Processes all files **locally** on your computer
- ✅ Does **NOT** send any data to external servers
- ✅ Does **NOT** track your usage
- ✅ Does **NOT** collect analytics
- ✅ Does **NOT** require an internet connection to function
- ✅ Does **NOT** access any files outside the folders you explicitly select

## How It Works

1. You select source and destination folders using your operating system's folder picker
2. The app processes files entirely on your local machine
3. All operations happen within your computer's file system
4. No data leaves your device

## File Access

The app only accesses:
- Files within the source folder you select
- The destination folder you select for output
- No other files or folders on your system

## Permissions

The app requires:
- **File System Access**: To read source files and write cleaned files to destination
- **No Network Access**: The app functions completely offline
